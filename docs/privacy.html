Privacy

No analytics. No cookies. No tracking. Here's exactly what happens to anything you type into this site.

Last updated: April 28, 2026 · v1.0

About this page

The Conference Team Skills repository at github.com/msg2ai/conference-team-skills is an open-source collection of Claude Skills released by MSG2AI. The landing page you're on is a single static HTML file served from docs/index.html in that repository — it has no backend, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking.

What we don't collect

  • We do not run any analytics service (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no PostHog, etc.).
  • We do not set cookies of any kind.
  • We do not log IP addresses, user agents, or referrers.
  • We do not embed third-party trackers, pixels, or fingerprinting scripts.

Third-party assets we load

For typography and a markdown renderer on the mock conference page, the site loads two scripts from public CDNs:

  • Google Fonts — for the Fraunces and Inter typefaces. Loading these pages may expose your IP to Google. See Google's privacy policy.
  • jsDelivr — to load marked.js on the mock conference page. See jsDelivr's privacy policy.

If you'd prefer not to be exposed to either, you can self-host the same files locally — see the repository.

The contact form

The contact form on the landing page never transmits anything to a server we control. When you press Send, your browser opens a mailto: link that launches your local email client with the contents pre-filled. The email itself is sent through whatever provider you use (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, etc.) and is governed by their privacy policy.

Once your email arrives at info@msg2ai.xyz, it is read by people at MSG2AI and stored in our email provider (currently Zoho Mail). We use it only to reply to you and respond to your inquiry. We don't add you to a marketing list. You can ask us to delete the email at any time by replying to the thread.

What about Claude itself?

The skills published in this repository are markdown briefs that run inside Claude or Claude Code. Anything you type into Claude is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy and Consumer Terms — not by us. The skills do not transmit your data anywhere on their own; they only act through tools you've explicitly connected (Gmail, Drive, Vercel, etc.), and what those tools do with your data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes

If we change this policy we'll update the version stamp at the top of this page. The full history is visible in the git log of the repository.
